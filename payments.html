Don't have cash? Want to pay online? We accept Paypal!

If you don't already have an account, sign up for free at Paypal.com

Go to https://www.paypal.com/paypalme/fcvfcevent or if logging into paypal directly our paypal account is: event@fordclifffire.org.

Be sure to select the friends and family option and add a note with your name so we know what the payment is for!

Still not sure how to do it? Follow the steps below or contact us.



Go to https://www.paypal.com/paypalme/fcvfcevent and Click on Send


Login with your paypal account or sign up for a new one


Select the amount and what the payment is for in on the note line


Pick the sending to someone you trust option


Pick your bank account to save us fees!


Click send payment now
