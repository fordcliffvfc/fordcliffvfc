The Ford Cliff Banquet Hall is the Perfect Venue for your next event!

The Ford Cliff Banquet Hall is a modern facility equipped with a bar, stage, and full kitchen to help make your event a success. We can accommodate approximately 200 people and have two parking lots to accommodate large groups. We can provide catering and bartenders for your event or you can provide your own.

Bartending Service Full Bar Available

The Ford Cliff Banquet Hall Bar has two taps, an ice machine, and a beverage cooler as well as seating at the bar. Whether you want a simple drink setup or full service we've got you covered!

Catering Full Kitchen Available

We can accomodate large groups with sit down service or buffets. The hall can be decorated and arranged to fit your groups needs.
