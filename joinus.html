Interested in Volunteering? Stop by any Monday night at 7PM

Whether you are experienced or inexperienced, young or old we have a job for you! It takes help from everyone to keep our trucks on the road and responding to our community when they need us most.

If you are interested in becoming a firefighter and don't have experience, we will send you through the PA State Fire academy essentials program.

If you are not interested in the fire side there is still plenty you can do! Helping at our events is a great way to meet new people and help your community. Stop in Mondays at 7PM or any time you see us around to see how you can help!
